African violet

Water
Jan 27. Feb 5, 15, 27
Min-max: 400-520 gr. Rec vol: ml.
High, warm
Keep soil moist with warm water. Don't allow water to have contact with leaves other than light misting. Water from below. Don't let the plants sit in water.
Fertilizer
Rose ¼: Feb 5, 27
biweekly, African violet fertilizer
Feed biweekly with an African violet fertilizer.

Arugula

Water
Jan 27. Feb 5, 15, 24
Min-max: 1530-2355 gr. Rec vol: 500 ml.
High
Regular watering. Water as soon as top inch feels dry. Keep soil consistently moist but not soggy. Fail to do this might cause plant to bolt and flavor ruined.
Fertilizer
Feb 15
Low, all purpose ½.
If planted in nitrogen rich soil, doesn't need additional feeding. If it's lack of nourishment, the leaves turn pale.
