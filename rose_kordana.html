Rose Kordana

A miniature rose

Purchased April 2022 (Easter) and May 2022 (I think)

The April one is mixed orange an d pink. The May one are red.

Journal 2022
  • April
    • 9: Purchased from Walmart, Easter edition. Mix pink (mostly) and reddish orange (just 1) color. transferred into white long window planter.
    • 27: transferred into 3 round pots:
      • 1 red in white small pot
      • 2 pinks in clay pot
      • 2 pinks and 1 pinkish in white porcelain pot
    • 28: thyme seeds sown on the bottom. 6, 6, and 7 holes.
  • May
    • 15: thyme seedlings are removed because it's hard to water gentle fot it and larger practical volume of roses.
    • 15: A half of the roses roses dried up to the begining of the stem. Their lives ended: red in white small pot and 1 of 2 of the pink in clay pot. THe other one is transefered to the white porcelain pot. Now they are all in the 1 pot. The flowers are all removed. Some turning into dry flower. The stems drying up. The pinkish one was in full bloom but start browning in the edges with spider mites. All dried foliages and stems are removed. Sprayed with insecticidal soap.
    • 17: Re. Spider mites. Washed leaves of suspected invested plants. Basil, kemangi, mini rose, bunnytails, pole beans, cucumber, tomatoes, and pumpkin. Removed a lot of yellowing leaves. Cilantro thrown away since it's just more practical to restart.
  • June
    • 4: Several buds observed. 2 Full blooms (red, from newer ones).
    • 5: Transferred outdoor together with candytuft, replacing campanula.
Care
  • Bright light - full sun.
  • Water when needed, don't let dry out.
  • Fertilize every other week.
