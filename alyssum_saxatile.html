Journal
  • 23 Jan 2022: Planted together with Alyssum carpet of snow.
  • 1 Feb: Seedlings emerging, 1-2 cm height
  • 8 Feb: Up tp 1 inch.
  • 12 Mar: about 10 cm.

  • 25 Mar: Watered thoroughly in preparation of 10 days holiday.
  • 3 Apr: Back from holiday, it looks not so good. Will watch for several days before deciding to keep or to let go.
  • 6 Apr: It seems like all the white one are dead. And most of the yellow one are fine. Removed all dead plants and dead foliages. Remove the small rock, top up with indoor mix.
  • On May, when the plants flower and they are white. So it was the other alyssum, the carpet of snow, that are alive. This one has been removed since 6 Apr.
