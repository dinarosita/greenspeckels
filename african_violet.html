Green Speckles

African Violet

Streptocarpus sect. Saintpaulia

About:

Native to Tanzania and Kenya. Flowers several times a year or throughout the year.

Updates:
  • Purchased I think October 2021 from Home Depot. It had some flowers.
  • 23 Jan 2022: After the first flowers went wilted and removed, it hasn't flowered again yet. But the foliage is bushier.
  • 8 Feb: Nothing has changed. Problem: Hasn't been flowering since maybe Nov.
  • 18 Feb: Budding!!
  • 1 Mar: There are 2 stems of buds. Each has 7 buds, and the top ones (2 of them in total) are quite large and about to bloom.
  • 9 Mar: Blooming profusely. 4 full bloom, 1 opening, more than 10 buds. Distributed in 3 flower stems.
  • 11 Mar: 6 full bloom, 15 buds, 1 more stem (bud not counted)
Care:
  • Bright but indirect sunlight.
  • High humidity, but don't let the leaves wet, other than light misting.
  • Water with warm water. Better to put the water spout deep in the soil when watering.
  • Good temperature is about 21 °C. Don't let it go under 15 °C.
  • Fertilize biweekly.
