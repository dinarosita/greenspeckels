Green Speckles

Afrian Violet

Streptocarpus sect. Saintpaulia

Updates:

Purchased I think October 2021 from Home Depot. It had some flowers.

23 January 2022: After the first flowers went wilted and removed, it hasn't flowered again yet. But the foliage is bushier.

About:

Native to Tanzania and Kenya.

Care:
  • Bright but indirect sunlight.
  • High humidity, but don't let the leaves wet, other than light misting.
  • Water with warm water. Better to put the water spout deep in the soil when watering.
  • Good temperature is about 21 °C. Don't let it go under 15 °C.
  • Fertilize biweekly.
