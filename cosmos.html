Package information
  • Burpee $2.99
  • Cosmos: Lemonade
  • Annual
  • Full sun, 40-45 days, 51 cm height, 0.6 cm depth, thin to 20-25 cm
  • Sow after danger of heavy frost is gone. 10 cm apart, keep evenly moist, seedlings emerge in 7-10 days. Thin when 2.5 cm height.
  • Container friendly, 3 plants per 40 cm container.
  • Bloom: summer to fall
Care
  • After established, drought resistant. Will be the last plant requiring water if water limited. Do not overwater. Overwatering leads to fewer blooms.
  • Love hot weather, thrive in any humidity level.
  • Do not fertilize unless struggling. Handle poor soil well. Fertilizer leads to strong plant with lots of foliages but few blooms.
