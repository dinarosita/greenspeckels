Oso Easy Italian Ice

Rose Oso Easy Italian Ice

A shrub rose with semi doubled blooms of a mix color: yellow, orange, and pink. 24-30 petals per bloom. Contionus bloom. Smells more gentle, but has yellow characteristic that I love.

Olivia said she likes this one better than the yellow Berolina last year. So it's justified, because I like the yellow Berolina better. It's yellow, smell very fragrant in yellow way, many rows or petal, bloom last long and strong, and it's larger. It's just, the only yellow rose with many row of petals i found are floribunda in walmart, that looks really bad.
