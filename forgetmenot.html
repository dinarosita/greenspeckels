Green Speckles

Forget-Me-Not

Cygnoglossum amabile

Firmament

Chinese Forget-Me-Not

Also called Chinese hound's tongue, native to Asia. Closely related to the common forget-me-not (Myosotis).

Planted May 2022

McKenzie seeds.

Journal 2022
  • May
    • 15: Sow. McKenzie seeds. 5 small jiffies with starting mix.
    • 20: Emerge
  • June
    • 2: Growing tall fast. Already 3 cm. Transferred to 5 larger jiffies.
    • 2: Plant 5 jiffies, 2 each.
Package information
  • McKenzie $1.89
  • Forget-Me-Not: Firmament
  • Annual
  • Full sun, 45 cm, cutflower.
  • Days to sprout: 7-14
  • Seed depth: 6 mm
  • Seed spacing: 2.5 cm
  • Spread: 20 cm
  • Bloom period: July - Aug
  • Plant directly in the garden after last frost.
  • Best in moist soil but can withstand most soil conditions.
Care
  • Sun to partial shade
  • Average water need. Water regularly, do not overwater. Water well until plants are 10-15 cm tall. After that point, reduce watering. In nature and gardens, it survives just with natural rain water.
  • Mild temperature
  • Cut back after flowering
  • Fertilizing: Maybe not needed. No reference mention it and it's invasive. So might be growing well easily as is.
Warnings
  • Toxicity: Parts of plant are poisonous if ingested.
  • Invasive, with seeds sticks to everythings. Even though this also means low maintenance.
  • Diseases: Powdery mildew - Don't plant to densely,five some space in between.
  • Pests: generally pest free
