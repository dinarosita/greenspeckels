Green Speckles

Cilantro (quick flower)

Coriandrum sativum

About:

Fast growing but short lived herbs. 2 months before it flowers. After flowers, herb taste not good anymore. This variety is the quick flowering one that's grown to collect the seeds (coriander seeds) rather than to eat the leaves. The leaves are ready to harvest from 3-4 weeks after planting.

Updates:

18 Dec 2021: Planted from seeds (McKenzie). Seeds package specified suickly flowering.

I think 28 Dec 2021: First seedling emerged.

31 Dec 2021: Almost all emerged. Some still folded and low (1.5cm), some already straight up (4-5 cm)

Was covered in paper towel, now cling wrap.

11 Jan 2022: About 7 cm. Pebbles added to the surface recently.

23 Jan: Getting bushier and about 10 cm tall.

9 Feb: Bushy and thriving. Maybe 15-20 cm long. Ready to be thinned/harvested a little bit. New location: Living room southeast window to give partial shade.

Care:
  • Fertilizer is not necessary, but if wanted, once a month. Date: 3 Feb
