Care:
  • Similar growing condition with thyme: excellent plant for hot, dry, and sunny places; it can thrive indoors given enough direct light.
  • Needs 3 months for reaching harvest maturity.
  • Full sun
  • Sandy loam. Allow soil to dry completely between watering. Do not over water. Only water thoroughy adter soil is dry to touch.
  • Do not fertilize. It can change the flavor.
  • Pinch back when it is 4 inches tall. As the plant grow larger, pinch back every week. If it's becoming too woody, cut back.
  • Harvest is best right before the plant flowers. But harvest anytime is good.
  • Seeds require some light to germinate.
