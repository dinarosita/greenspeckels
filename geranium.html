Package information
  • McKenzie $4.99
  • Geranium, Red Zonale F1, annual
  • Full sun, 35 cm, Start early
  • Start indoor 10-14 weeks before the last frost.
  • Pinch back to encourage bushy form. Remove faded blooms to encourage blooming.
  • Days to sprout 7-28
  • Seed depth 3 mm
  • Seed spacing 2.5 cm
  • SPread 30 cm
  • Bloom period June- frost
Care
  • Water: deeply. At least weekly. Might need daily when hot.
  • Fertilizer: every 4-6 weeks, 5-10-5.
  • Repot when overgrown. Noted by wilting between watering.
