Journal 2022

Package information
  • Burpee, $1.99
  • Chives: Dolores
  • Full sun, +6 h
  • 80-90 days
  • 30-46 cm height
  • Sow in full sun after danger of frost is gone. Emerge in 7-14 days. 0.6 cm depth.
  • Thin to 8 cm apart when seedlings are about 2.5-5 cm high.
  • Container friendly. 3 plants per 30 cm container.
Care
  • Fertilizing: low dose. Water soluble half strength, twice a month. Heavier does might weakeng the taste.
  • Keep humidity high. Wet pebble tray, daily mist.
  • Watering high. Water when top of soil is dry to the touch.
