Package information
  • McKenzie $4.99
  • Petunia: Blue Easy Wave
  • Annual
  • Full sun, 15-30 cm, start early, AAS winner.
  • Days to sprout: 7-21
  • Seed depth: 3 mm
  • Seed spacing: 1.3 cm
  • Spread: <150 cm
  • Bloom period: June - frost
  • Start indoor 6-8 weeks before last frost.
Care
  • Regular watering and as much sunlight as possible to promote multiple blooms.
  • Spreading type is picky about water. Weekly watering is minimum
  • Tolerate partially shaded, but fuller and more abundant in full sun.
  • Fertilization: balanced water soluble fertilizer for blooming plants. Heavy feeder. Weekly feeding.
  • Deadhead. If seeds are allowed, blooming will slow down to stopping.
