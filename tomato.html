Package information
  • McKenzie, $3.89
  • Tomato, Tumbler F1 Hybrid (trailing)
  • 55 days, start early
  • Start indoor 6-8 weeks before last frost
  • Days to sprout: 7-14
  • Seed depth: 6 mm
  • Seed spacing: 2.5 cm
  • Plant spacing: 60-90 cm
  • Row spacing: 90 cm
Care
  • Fertilizer: Use tomato fertilizer, follow label.
  • Water: deep and regularly. Never let soil dry out. Overwatering when fruit developes can cause split.
