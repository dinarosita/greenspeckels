Green Speckles

Dianthus 'Maiden Pink'

Dianthus deltoides

About:

15-29 cm flowering evergreen perennials Native to Asia and Europe. Excellent groundcover.

Life:

15 May 2022 - now

Updates:
  • 15 May 2022: Sow. McKenzie seeds. 5 small jiffies with starting mix.
Care:
  • Full sun.
  • Neutral to alkaline, well-drained soil.
  • Versatile. Deer and drought resistant.
  • Deadhead to prolong flowering. Shear after flowering to keep neat.
  • Low to average water need.
