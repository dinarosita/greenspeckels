Dianthus 'Maiden Pink'

Dianthus deltoides

Dianthus Maiden Pink

45 cm flowering evergreen perennials Native to Asia and Europe. Excellent groundcover. Flowers are usually pink, and sometimes white or spotted white.

Planted May 2022

McKenzie seeds

Care
  • Full sun.
  • Neutral to alkaline, well-drained soil.
  • Versatile. Deer and drought resistant.
  • Deadhead to prolong flowering. Shear after flowering to keep neat.
  • Low to average water need.
