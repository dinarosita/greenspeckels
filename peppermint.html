Peppermint

Peppermint, also known as Mentha balsamea Wild, is a hybrid mint between watermint and spearmint. Indigenous to Europe and Middle East. Occasionally found wild with its parent species.

Care
  • Light: 6 hours of sunlight daily. Shade from strong afternoon sun.
  • Water: Keep consistently moist. Water when the top inch feels dry to touch.
  • Pruning: Pinch regularly to prmote bushy growth. If it gets too scraggly, cut back 1 inch aboe soil line to regenerate with fuller growth. Remove flower stalk because it degrades flavor.
  • Fertilizer: Avoid. Or if must: Feed once with 10-10-10 when new growth emerges. Then one more mid way growing season if nedeed.
