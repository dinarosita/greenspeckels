Lavatera trimestris: Twins Hot Pink

Common names: annual mallow, rose mallow, royal mallow, regal mallow, and common annual tree mallow. Alternative latin names: Malva trimestris and Althaea trimestris. Flowering plant native to Mediterranean. Typically grow quite tall. The Twins Hot Pink breed is a compact form: 30 cm height.

Planted May 2022

McKenzie seeds.

Package information

  • Lavatera: Twins Hot Pink
  • Annual
  • Full sun, 30-50 cm, container, fleuro select.
  • Days to sprout: 10-21
  • Seed depth: 6 mm
  • Seed spacing: 5 cm
  • Spread: 30 cm
  • Bloom period: July - frost
  • Start indoor 6-8 weeks before last frost for earlier bloom.
  • Performs best in sunny areas where it doesn't have to compete with vigorous neighbouring plants.
Care
  • Full sun
  • Light, well-drained soil, sandy, loamy
  • Exposure: sheltered
  • Deadhead
  • Diseases to be watched out:
    • Rust
  • Note from other Lavatera trimestris:
    • Water regularly. The plant is drought tolerant, but benefit from regular watering especially during hot period. Blooms will drop if soil gets too dry.
    • General-purpose fertilizer every month. Too much fertilizer may produce green leafy plant at the expense of blooms.
