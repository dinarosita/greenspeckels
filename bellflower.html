Purple Get MEE

Bellflower

Campanula is latin for little bell. Named for its bell shaped flowers. Get Mee variety is blooming from July to September. Versatile for ground cover, thrives in tight spaces, rock garden, and hanging baskets. Easy to care for, grow fast, and will spread.

Since May 2022

Purchased 2 from Home Depot, 12 May 2022. Young, low and wide, full of purple blooms, in 10 cm containers.
