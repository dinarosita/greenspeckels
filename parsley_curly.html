Parsley (Champion Moss Curled)

Petroselinum crispum

About:

Curly parsley is grown more for decoration than cooking.

Updates:

1 Jan 2022: Seeds from the same bag planted to a small pot.

23 Jan: Seedlings are 2-3 cm.

13 Feb: Thriving and bushy, above 5cm.
