Green Speckles

Candytuft (White Snow)

Iberis sempervirens

About:

Native to Mediterannean, flowering evergreen to perennial from mistard family. Semi-evergreen in our cold climate of zone 5. Beautiful flowers but with unpleasant scent.

Updates:

30 Dec 2021: Seeds (McKenzie) planted in new soil (MiracleGro potting mix), with rocks at the bottom of the pot. Covered in paper towel to avoid fungus gnat investation.

11 January 2022: Seedling started coming out few days ago. They are now ranging from 1 to 4 cm height, with 2 cm average.

23 Jan: 5 cm tall
