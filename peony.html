Care
  • Fertilize once or twice a year. With rose/bloom/bulb fertilizer once. In ring (not too close). Crown and toung growth are susceptible to burn. When? First recommendation, when it is 30 cm tall or leaves unfurl or buds forming. Other recommendation, when new growth poking through the soil. Also in the fall, after cutting back the plant.
  • Water: consistent watering. Drought tolerant for a short period of time, but best for growth and healthy root is consistent watering. 1 inch per week. Touch the soil for hot summer. In spring and fall, check if soil dry in secnod knuckle of finger dipped. Wilting, dropping buds, discolored, dried foliage are sign needing watering. Deep water every 10-14 days for mature plant, twice as much for young plants.
