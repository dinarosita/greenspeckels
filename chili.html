Chili

The beginning:

Planted from seeds from 1 Thai bird eye chili from grocery store in spring or early summer 2021.

Care:

Fertilize every 2 week (last Christmas weekend)

A little story:

Planted from 1 chili pepper in spring or early summer 2021. Majority of the seeds grew into seedlings, there were so many. Unfortunately due to space limitation, only less than 10 is kept. Grow so fast during the peak of summer into a meter tall.

Hand polinated. First green chili was seen I think September 2021. They grow in size quite fast, but remain green for a long time. First observed red ripe chili was December.

Second Home generation intitiated on 22 December 2021 from one overripe red chili with soft brown tip.
