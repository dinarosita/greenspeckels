Ellwood Cypress

Chamaecyparis lawsoniana

The beginning:

Purchased before 14 December 2021 from Home Depot, 28 cm height. I wonder how fast (how slow) it grows.

About:

Conifer. False-cypress. Slow growing evergreen tree, can reach 4-8m height in 20-50 years.

Care:

  • Full sun to partial shade.
  • Average, moist, well-drained soil.
  • Low maintenance, no pruning required.

References:

  • American Conifer Society: Chamaecyparis lawsoniana 'Ellwoodii' / Ellwood's Lawson cypress
