Care
  • Water: regular watering in first years, more drought tolerance in the next years.
  • Temperature and humidity: tolerant to summer heat and humidity. Can survive cold winter if soil is not too wet.
  • Fertilizer: not a heavy feeder, but if soil is poor, add flower fertilizer each spring as active growth begins. Will do fine if soil is decent.
