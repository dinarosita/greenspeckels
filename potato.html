Green Speckles

Potato

Solanum tuberosum

The story:

Early Dec: Tried to grow potatoes with eye buds in water, ended up turning black.

18 Dec: Chose 3 small potatoes with eyes and Olivia planted 2 with smaller eyes, Harrison planted 1 with bigger one, 1.5 cm long stem. All of them, including the bigger stem, is covered until soil surface.

They grow fast. By 24 Dec, the middle one already 1.5 cm above the ground.

By 26 Dec, the smaller ones in right and left are seen above the ground too.the right one has multiple stems. Some roots also grow above the soil level. might raise the soil level.

30 Dec left to right: 2 cm single, 5 cm single, 3 cm 5 stems. has been kept under plastic wrap to combat fungus gnat. Unfortunately start from today, several fungus gnats are observed inside the dome. Sign of investation. Have to deal with it.
