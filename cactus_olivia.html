Olivia's Cactus

Gymnocalycium mihanovichii

About

Moon cactus, Gymnocalycium mihanovichii. or Hibotan cactus. Native to deserts in South America. A mutant cactus that can't produce chlorophyll itself. It means It must be grafted to a rootstock cactus as its base. Rather short lived, that can be prolonged by re-grafting it into a new rootsctock.
