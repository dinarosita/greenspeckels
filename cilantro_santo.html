Cilantro (Slow Bolt)

Coriandrum sativum

About:

'Santo' variety: standard cilantro. Good bolt-resistance. Matures in 50-55 days.

The journey:

  • Planted from seeds 18 December 2021. West Coast Seeds, Santo Monogerm.
  • Was covered with paper towel to reduce risk of fungus gnat investation.
  • First noticed sprouting was 25-26 December. It's the type that folded half then straightened up, so it feels like growing very fast. Just on 27 December, the tallest one already reached 5 cm height. The covering is changed into cling wrap dome to allow sunlight.

Harvest information:

Pick young leaves when the plants reaching 15 cm height, about 3-4 weeks after seeding. Don't harvest more than 1/3 of the leaves at a time.

Care:

  • Prefer cooler weather. 15-21 °C. Will bolt if it gets too dry or too hot.
  • Full sun to partial shade. 6 hours of direct sunlight, but avoid harsh sunlight for too long. Will cause bolting.
  • Moist but not soggy. Will cause bolting.
    • Do not like high humidity or too much rain.
  • Doesn't need fertilizing. Monthly doesn't hurt.
  • Pruning: pretty much by harvesting is enough.
