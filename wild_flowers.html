Green Speckles

Wild Flower

The beginning:

Rescued from the sidewalk late autumn 2021 when the temperature started to drop below 0 °C, together with 3 other types of weeds. They looked beautiful and weather didn't beat them up yet. Dandelion-like flowers were blooming and some turning into seed puffs. Brought home mainly for Olivia. It was the day she fell on her nose and forehead. She likes the soft seed puffs. Kept indoor.

The end:

After the seed puffs dispersed, the arrangement was mostly wilted. They are then sacrificed. RIP wild flowers.
