Green Speckles

Polka Dot Plant

Hypoestes phyllostachya

Updates:

21 Dec 2021: Purchased from Home Depot.

23 Jan 2022: Has been growing well without issue at lot. Easy maintenance. Soil is covered in pebbles.

3 Feb: Deep watering with half outdoor dose all purpose fertilizer. Went to emergency care mode today. Plants that were find yesterday, today looks wilted. Soil was dry, probably that was the main problem. But also noticed is bright green color in the tip of a leave. Sign of too much sun? It has been put closer to the window more than usual recently.

4 Feb: Plants are leggy, did pinching, only the top sets.

x Feb: Pinching some more.

12 Feb: Pinching come more to 6 cm height. Heart breaking because removing growing buds from previous pinching. But they were really leggy still. Hopefullt not too much shock and kill the plants.

13 Feb: Moved back to south window to allow more sunlight since it was just pruned so heavily.

About:

Native to Madagascar

Care:
  • Humidity high, nondirect sunlight.
  • Fertilizer: heavy feeder, once a month. Dates: 3 Feb.
